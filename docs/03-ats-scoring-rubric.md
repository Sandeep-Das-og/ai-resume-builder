# ATS Scoring Rubric (Hybrid)

## Overview
Hybrid scoring = rules-based checks + AI evaluation. Total score out of 100.

## Rules-Based Score (60 points)
1. Structure & Sections (15)
- Must have: Summary/Objective, Experience, Skills, Education.
- Optional: Projects, Certifications, Awards.

2. Formatting & ATS Safety (15)
- Single column layout.
- Standard fonts, consistent sizes.
- No tables, text boxes, or images.
- Consistent bullet style and spacing.

3. Keyword Coverage (15)
- If job description provided, compute overlap on skills and role terms.
- If not provided, check against a standard skill taxonomy for the target role.

4. Contact & Metadata (5)
- Email and phone present.
- Location included (city/country). LinkedIn optional.

5. Quantified Impact (10)
- Experience bullets with metrics (%, $, time saved, scale).

## AI Score (40 points)
1. Clarity & Readability (15)
- Concise summary.
- Active voice.
- Avoid redundancy.

2. Role Fit & Story (15)
- Alignment with target role.
- Career progression and relevance.

3. Improvement Potential (10)
- Gaps or missing skills.
- Suggestions for stronger phrasing.

## Output
- Final score: 0–100.
- Section-by-section breakdown.
- Top 5 prioritized fixes.

## Optional Inputs
- Target role.
- Job description text.
